About Rongai Poultry

Rongai Poultry is a leader in animal husbandry, specializing in hatchery farming and biosafety training. We enhance poultry production through advanced hatchery techniques, ensuring the supply of high-quality chicks. Our commitment to biosafety protects against disease outbreaks, safeguarding both livestock and food security.

We provide comprehensive training programs for farmers, equipping them with the knowledge and skills needed for effective poultry management and biosecurity practices. Our after-sales support ensures that clients receive ongoing assistance, fostering long-term success and sustainability in poultry farming.
