At Rongai Poultry

Rongai Poultry specializes in providing comprehensive training and support for hatchery management and animal husbandry. Our services include expert guidance on optimizing hatchery operations, ensuring high-quality poultry production, and maintaining effective biosafety measures. We aim to enhance your knowledge and skills to ensure the best practices in poultry management.

Our training programs cover essential aspects of animal husbandry, including the latest techniques in poultry care, disease prevention, and biosecurity protocols. We are dedicated to helping you achieve operational excellence and maximize the productivity and health of your poultry.

At Rongai Poultry, we are committed to delivering practical and actionable insights that contribute to successful poultry farming. Our goal is to support you in creating a thriving and sustainable hatchery environment while adhering to the highest standards of animal welfare and biosafety.
